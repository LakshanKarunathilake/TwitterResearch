TREND SPOTTER

Trend spotter is the unique app which you can use to identify trends and insights building around a brand. Trend spotter is the result of a collaborative research done by four final year students and Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT).

You can use the ‘search’ button on the menu bar to search for a twitter use. And on the relevant result use can click ‘subscribe’ to add that account to the monitoring list. On the Menu bar you can use the ‘reports’ button to access the reports of your monitoring accounts. And the ‘settings’ button can be used to unsubscribe from the subscribed accounts. We currently support up to five twitter accounts to be monitored.
